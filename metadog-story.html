Andy's Story

The founders of MetaaDog love dogs and they want to do something for children with serious illnesses. We invited over 70 artists to a contest for dog character design. The requirement was the dog image must be cute, pleasant to view. And it must be simple in design. There were over 150 submissions, which are all of very high quality. However, Andy’s design stands out. Andy is a very talented artist. His art is simple, vivid, and has a soul in it. Andy is a dog lover too. The MetaaDog is a humanized version of his labrador puppy. Below was Andy’s original art design. We believe this is the image that will be loved by children, families, and our community. Since then Andy has joined the MetaaDog team and has become an important member of this loving project.

What you did for helping families with sick children?

Metlab Technology Ltd, which is the operating vehicle for MetaaDog operates in New Zealand. We have been working with Ronald McDonald House (RMH NZ), an iconic organization for helping families with sick kids. Every year we participate in activities for RMH such as street appeal, annual Christmas party cooking for children there.

For street appeal, we stand on the street to collect donations for RMH, which also increases the awareness of RMH. For the Christmas parties, we bring food and cook BBQ for the children who stay at RMH during Christmas. We really enjoyed what we did there. IT does make your heart harm when you are there.
