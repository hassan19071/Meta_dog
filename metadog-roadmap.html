Our Roadmap

Home | Roadmap

Releasing of 2D NFT

This is the start of building our ecosystem. This phase sets the foundation of our philosophies. MetaaDog intends to bring smiles and happiness to people and their families. Loving and caring are our core values. Hence, our designs tend to be cute and funny. Unlike many other NFTs, which are created with a wild imagination, our design tries to link metaverse to reality. So, the designs are close to real life and family-focused.

Phase 01

Phase 02

Releasing 3D NFT and Utilities

Our 3D NFT is animated with extremely high quality, which makes it more fun to collect. The mint price will be much higher than 2D. It is designed to increase the depth of community and the values of 2D NFT. 3D NFT will be utilities-enabled, which will include Airdrop and $MD token. The $MD token will be used in our merchant store and games.

Market MetaaDog Toys and Other Merchants

We genuinely believe for an NFT brand to have a long life; it needs to become well-known in real life, not only in the Crypto or NFT world. We will be manufacturing Metaadog Toys and other merchants, and we will be actively marketing them offline. In phases 6 and 7 of our roadmap, a more extensive and aggressive plan will be used to reach people outside of the crypto world.

Phase 04

MetaaDog Mini-Game

Before the launch of Metaverse Games, we will release our mini-games, which serve as a bridge to fill the long development period for the Metaverse game. These are free games, which will allow a broader audience to become aware of our brand and enjoy what MetaaDog provides at their pleasure. We believe this will be a great way to enhance our brand outside of the crypto world.

Metaverse Game

The current design of our Metaverse game is a non-violent but fun game that is built on our own Metaverse. It will be the first family-focused game. This will be a masterpiece of the MetaaDog brand, which will require cornerstone venture capital investors. We are currently talking with some of the investors.

Phase 06

MetaaDog Cartoon Book Series

In this phase, we start our cartoon book series. Each episode tells a story about MetaaDog characters. It could be about the mom, dad, boy, and girl. We hope to produce a cartoon book series as good as the classic ones like TinTin. Each of the MetaaDog episodes will be an NFT game on its own.

MetaaDog TV series

Provided that the previous 6 phases are successful, this will be the natural phase of the development of MetaaDog brand. We are trying to put art, metaverse, and real-life nicely together, and constantly develop our brand both online and offline.

Donation

Donations Activity and Announcement

The donations to charities will be published throughout the phases. It is important for the community to understand that we have committed 20% of our annual net profit to charities, which may negatively affect our NFT and token value in the short run because a significant amount of money will be moved out from our community. However, this is part of MetaaDog’s mission. And we believe it will increase the value of MetaaDog assets in the long run.

Profit Charing Scheme Overview

MetaaDog is community-focused. Bringing value to our community members is one of MetaaDog’s missions. Apart from the charity contribution mentioned above, our NFT holders will enjoy dividends each year, guaranteed at 20% of the net profit of the year. 50% of the total profit will be reinvested back in the project. The staff of MetaaDog, will also be rewarded with bonuses. The actual amount of profit being shared, charity contribution, and staff bonus will be announced at the end of the New Zealand financial year.
