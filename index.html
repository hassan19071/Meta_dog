What are MetaaDogs?

MetaaDogs are a collection of programmatically generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. It consists of 10,000 2D dog images and 8000 3D dog animations. The 2D NFTs are hand-picked from over 5 trillion total possible options. It has a variety of outfits, expressions, accessories, and colors. Each Metaadog consists of a unique combination of these properties. The 3D dog animations have 16 different types of actions with a variety of emotions and backgrounds. These types of animation designs have not been seen in the NFT world yet.

Meet MetaaDog Characters

MetaaDog has 4 characters with different colors. 2 are females and 2 are males. It represents mom, dad, boy, and girl. However, both female and male colors can be adult or children. The dark colors are for males and the light colors are for females. NFT images are built on these 4 basic characters.
