What's Metaadog Vision

MetaaDog strives to become an iconic name that is known to the world for helping families with seriously ill children.

We believe NFTs will only have a long life when it is a part of real life. MetaaDog starts from an NFT but it will play an active role in real life. We try to link art, metaverse, and real-life nicely together by creatively developing MetaaDog brand both online and offline. Our future will witness the history of the creation and evolution of a long-lasting brand that is well-known in both the Metaverse and the real world.
